Good to Know

Do I need to read a series to start?

No. The Beginning is book one and is a complete, self-contained story. It also sets up a trilogy, so there's plenty more if you love it.

What genre is it?

A contemporary thriller with a real-time, countdown structure — think disaster-thriller tension with a techno edge. Fast-paced and plot-driven.

Is it appropriate for all readers?

It's written for adult and older-teen thriller fans. Expect suspense and peril, but nothing gratuitous.
