The Premise

When the lights go out,
the clock starts.

At 9:14 on an ordinary Tuesday, every screen, signal, and circuit in Manhattan dies at once. It isn't an accident. It's a message — and a deadline. Zero Hour: New York is the explosive first book in a thriller series about the ninety minutes that change everything, and the people forced to decide what they'll do when the rules disappear.
