About the Book

A City Fractures by Design

When a coordinated blackout freezes the city in seconds, the failure exposes more than vulnerable infrastructure. It reveals a system stretched past the point of trust, a population pushed beyond the point of patience, and a power struggle already underway beneath the streets. Zero Hour: New York is a fast-paced political thriller about power, pressure, and the dangerous moment when a city can no longer pretend everything is fine.
